Frequently Asked

Got questions?
We've got answers.

How does the transaction commission work?
Fewsion charges 10–15% on every completed project. This is paid by the brand on top of the creator's agreed fee — creators and editors keep their full quoted amount.
Do creators and editors pay to join?
No. Creator and Editor Free tiers are genuinely free — no hidden charges. Brands pay the platform commission. Creators and editors can upgrade to Pro for extra visibility, but it's never required.
How does escrow work exactly?
When a brand approves a creator for a campaign, they deposit the full agreed amount via Razorpay into escrow. This is held safely until the creator delivers approved content. No content, no release.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. All subscriptions are month-to-month with no lock-in. You can downgrade or cancel at any time — you keep access until the end of the billing period.
Is there a minimum spend for brands?
None at all. You can post a campaign with a budget as low as ₹5,000. Fewsion is built for D2C brands of all sizes, including those just starting with UGC.
When does my Creator or Editor get paid?
Escrow releases within 24 hours of brand approval. Payment hits the creator or editor's linked bank account or UPI within 1–2 business days via Razorpay.
